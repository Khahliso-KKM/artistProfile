About Me

I am an artist in the music industry, focused on song-writing, making music and performing. I love RnB as a genre, so I'd say it qualifies as my current favourite, but I do indulge in other genres, particularly Hip hop, House & Amapiano.

The in-between pauses within the journey gave me time to explore some of my interest or curiousity rather. So I learnt how to DJ, and some of the genres I'm capable of mixing include House, Electro House, Amapiano, RnB, Hip Hop & Afro Beat.
